Welcome!

Thank you for playing Ball Chaser World Tour! This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles information. Our game is a fun, action-packed adventure designed for children up to 7 years old — drag the ball around the world to escape animals, visit 35 capitals, and become the Grand World Champion!

🔒 We are committed to protecting children's privacy. We do not collect, store, share, or sell any personal data — from children or anyone else.
🚫

No Data Collection — Ever

Our game does not collect any information, including:

  • No names, usernames, or email addresses
  • No location or GPS data
  • No device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • No photos, contacts, or files from your device
  • No usage analytics or behavioral data
  • No cookies or tracking technologies
  • No crash reports containing personal information

The game runs entirely on your device. No data is transmitted to us or any third party.

👶

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

This app is designed for children under the age of 7 and complies fully with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

  • We do not direct any data collection at children.
  • We do not display any advertisements.
  • We do not include in-app purchases.
  • We do not require an account or sign-in.
  • We do not include any social features, chat, or sharing.
  • We do not use third-party SDKs that collect data.
🎮

Ads & In-App Purchases

This game contains no advertisements of any kind and no in-app purchases. There are no links to external websites, social networks, or third-party services within the app. It is a completely self-contained, offline game.

📱

App Permissions

Our game requests no special permissions on your device. It does not access the internet, camera, microphone, contacts, storage, or any other sensitive device feature. It is a simple tap-and-play game that works fully offline.

🤝

Third-Party Services

We do not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising networks, social login services, or data processors into this app. No data is ever shared with any third party because no data is collected in the first place.

The only platform involved is the Google Play Store, which may have its own data practices as the distribution platform. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details about their services.

📝

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this Privacy Policy, we will post the new version on this page and update the "Last Updated" date at the top. Since we do not collect any contact information, we cannot notify users directly — so we encourage parents and guardians to review this page occasionally.

Any future version of the app that introduces data collection will include a prominent update to this policy and will comply fully with all applicable laws and Google Play policies before release.

🕹️

Instructions — How to Play

  • 🎯 A ball appears on screen. An animal will chase it — touch and HOLD the ball with your finger, then DRAG it around to escape. Warning: if any animal touches the ball — even while you hold it — the game is over!
  • ⏱️ Survive for 60 seconds without being caught to complete the level and move to the next country.
  • ⚡ At 45 seconds remaining a second animal appears — it moves randomly (not chasing). At 30 seconds a third one joins too!
  • 📊 Difficulty sets how many balls you must protect: Easy = 1 ball, Medium = 2 balls, Hard = 3 balls. All balls must survive!
  • 🎚️ In Settings you can control: Speed (0–100) of the chaser, Ball Size (Small / Medium / Big) and Animal Size (Small / Medium / Big). Default size is Big.
  • 🌍 Each level takes you to a new world capital with a famous landmark as the background.
  • ✈️ After each level a plane flies you to the next destination — see where you are heading!
  • 💾 Your progress is automatically saved after every level — you can always continue from where you left off.
  • 🏆 Complete all 35 world capitals to become the Grand World Champion and receive the Golden Globe Trophy!
